Our Mission

Welcome to Paper Tiger Media! Our goal is to inform you about anything and everything related to sports. Because to us, the season never ends.

Why Paper Tiger?

The name "Paper Tiger" represents teams and players who look dominant on paper but struggle when it matters most. We break down the hype vs. the reality, delivering in-depth coverage of the NFL, NBA, and many other sports. Whether it’s draft prospects, trade rumors, game recaps, or analysis, we cut through the noise to bring you the real story. Sports are about more than just stats and highlight; They’re about narratives, rivalries, and unforgettable moments—and we’re here to cover it all. Join us as we analyze, debate, and celebrate the games we love.
