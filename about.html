Our Mission

Welcome to Paper Tiger Media! Our goal is to provide unbiased, nonpartisan political commentary and offer meaningful discussions on the issues that affect you, an American. Paper Tiger Media is stands on the principles of doing everything we can to provide you with the correct information, and is for the people, by the people, and of the people.

Why Paper Tiger?

The name “Paper Tiger” reflects the concept of democracy. A paper tiger is something that seems strong and menacing. However, it can crumble at any moment. Paper Tiger's goal is to expel misinformation and inform you.
