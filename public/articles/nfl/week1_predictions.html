The 2025 NFL season kicks off with Cowboys vs Eagles on Thursday Night Football. From divisional rivalries to rookie debuts and primetime clashes, here are our full predictions for Week 1 across the league.

All rights go to the NFL and respective teams

Cowboys @ Eagles

Prediction: Eagles 28, Cowboys 14

Philly has home field advantage and the more complete roster. Saquon Barkley will carry the load offensively with two touchdowns. Dallas may strike early, but the post-Micah Parsons defense won’t hold up for four quarters.

ML: Eagles (-) | Spread: Eagles -7

Chiefs @ Chargers (Friday Night)

Prediction: Chargers 24, Chiefs 21

The Chargers shock the NFL with an early upset. Patrick Mahomes and the Chiefs are still elite, but Justin Herbert looks comfortable in Greg Roman’s offense. Expect WR Ladd McConkey to shine under the primetime lights.

Dolphins @ Colts

Prediction: Dolphins 31, Colts 19

Daniel Jones starts for Indianapolis, but Miami has too much talent on both sides of the ball. Anthony Richardson’s benching casts a shadow over Indy’s season before it even starts.

Steelers @ Jets

Prediction: Steelers 16, Jets 10

Defense dominates in East Rutherford. The Jets begin the Justin Fields era, but Mike Tomlin’s Steelers grind out a tough win.

Panthers @ Jaguars

Prediction: Panthers 28, Jaguars 24

Bryce Young looks sharper in Year 2, connecting with rookie WR Tet McMillan. Carolina edges Jacksonville in a coaching chess match.

Cardinals @ Saints

Prediction: Cardinals 21, Saints 16

Kyler Murray leads Arizona to a scrappy road win. Spencer Rattler struggles in his Saints debut.

Giants @ Commanders

Prediction: Commanders 34, Giants 12

Washington, fresh off an NFC Championship run, rolls with Jayden Daniels. Russell Wilson’s Giants debut is a rough one.

Buccaneers @ Falcons

Prediction: Buccaneers 34, Falcons 31

Michael Penix Jr. impresses in his NFL debut, but Baker Mayfield and Tampa Bay steal the game with a Chase McLaughlin field goal.

Bengals @ Browns

Prediction: Bengals 38, Browns 22

Joe Burrow looks locked in while Cleveland’s revolving door at quarterback continues. Joe Flacco, at 40, won’t be enough.

Raiders @ Patriots

Prediction: Patriots 27, Raiders 17

Drake Maye enters Year 2 with confidence as Mike Vrabel returns to the sidelines. Geno Smith and the Raiders keep it close early, but New England pulls away.

49ers @ Seahawks

Prediction: 49ers 35, Seahawks 24

Sam Darnold reunites with his old team, but San Francisco’s depth and defense prove too much.

Titans @ Broncos

Prediction: Broncos 21, Titans 10

Bo Nix and Denver’s defense suffocate rookie QB Cam Ward in his first start.

Lions @ Packers

Prediction: Lions 26, Packers 22

Two NFC North contenders clash. Jordan Love looks strong, but Detroit’s balance and toughness carry them to victory.

Texans @ Rams

Prediction: Texans 40, Rams 32

CJ Stroud rebounds from his sophomore slump with a statement win. Expect a shootout, but Houston’s young core delivers.

Ravens @ Bills (Sunday Night Football)

Prediction: Bills 28, Ravens 24

Lamar Jackson vs Josh Allen in primetime delivers fireworks. Buffalo’s home-field advantage tips the balance.

Vikings @ Bears (Monday Night Football)

Prediction: Bears 24, Vikings 14

JJ McCarthy makes his long-awaited debut after last year’s ACL injury, but Caleb Williams and Rome Odunze steal the show in Chicago.

Conclusion

Week 1 of the 2025 NFL season is packed with storylines: Barkley’s Eagles debut, rookie quarterbacks under pressure, and heavyweight clashes on primetime. Follow along all season with Paper Tiger Media and join the conversation on Twitter @PaperTigersMedia.
