The second day of the 2025 NFL Draft kicks off tonight at 6 PM CT from Green Bay, Wisconsin. The Cleveland Browns hold the first pick of the second round; Houston Texans have the second pick after a trade with the New York Giants. Here, we will break down surprises and trades, and have every pick listed. This article will be frequently updated.

All rights go to NFL and the teams

The second and third rounds of the 2025 NFL Draft start today at 6 PM CT, or 7 PM ET. Notable players like Shedeur Sanders, Nick Emmanwori, Will Johnson, Mike Green, and Luther Burden are still on the board.

Updates: Nick Emmanwori was drafted by the Seattle Seahawks at 35. Luther Burden was drafted 39th overall by the Chicago Bears. Will Johnson went to the Arizona Cardinals at 47. Mike Green was selected to the Ravens at 59.

Surprises

Colorado QB Shedeur Sanders is still undrafted. He may even fall out of the second round. Louisville QB Tyler Shough, who is 25 years old, got drafted to the Saints at 40. Mike Green, who led the nation in sacks and had multiple sexual assault allegations, went to the Ravens at 59. Green will add to that already impressive Ravens defense.
